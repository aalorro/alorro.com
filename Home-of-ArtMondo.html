DIGITAL CANVAS

Binary Brush

Where art meets Ctrl+Z. 

Binary Brush dives into the curious romance between creativity and code, celebrating the digital artist’s world of layers, masks, and perfectly imperfect pixels. Here, every brushstroke is a byte, every glitch a happy accident, and the undo button is just as sacred as inspiration itself. Binary Brush is a tongue-in-cheek tribute to making magic with a stylus and screen—where the canvas is infinite, and the only limit is your RAM.

STREET ART, 2025

vision

To discover and share the emotional depth and beauty of the everyday world through digital artistry. 

As I transition from decades of engineering precision to creative exploration, my vision is to blend technology and imagination—capturing the essence of life, memory, and movement in digital form. I seek to create a body of work that inspires, evokes emotion, and connects with people beyond words. I believe that it's never too late to reinvent yourself—and that the intersection of art and technology holds limitless possibilities.

mission

To continuously explore and express my artistic voice through digital mediums, using tools like my Samsung Galaxy tablet to create works that are bold, personal, and experimental. 

Drawing from a lifelong passion for photography and a career in technology, I aim to honor both logic and spontaneity in my art—embracing the intuitive and imperfect. My mission is to connect with viewers, fellow artists, and art lovers—sharing my evolution as an artist and encouraging others to follow their own creative paths, especially those starting later in life.
